The Project

Four Dashboards. One Story.

Each dashboard surfaces a different layer of logistics operations — from warehouse floor KPIs to carrier performance and cost efficiency — designed for both operational teams and senior stakeholders.

01

Warehouse KPI Dashboard

Tracks the core operational health of three Doncaster-area warehouses across 12 months — monitoring order fulfilment rate, pick accuracy, units per hour, dock-to-stock time, and SLA compliance broken down by warehouse and shift.

Key Insight
WH-DON-02 shows a fulfilment rate dip to ~87% in April–May, clearly visible in the multi-line trend chart. This operational anomaly would trigger a targeted review in a real 3PL environment.
94.74%Avg Fulfilment Rate
98.50%Avg Pick Accuracy
88.5Avg Units/Hour
02

Inventory Forecasting Dashboard

Monitors stock health across 80 SKUs and 6 product categories, tracking stockout risk, overstock flags, days of stock coverage, and supply vs demand balance over a 12-month window.

Key Insight
Chilled category experiences a stockout risk spike in March, driven by demand running ~60% above normal. Apparel carries the highest persistent stockout risk across the year, suggesting reorder point recalibration is needed.
885KTotal Stock Units
24KSKUs at Risk
123SKUs Overstocked
03

Supply Chain Delay Analysis

Analyses carrier performance across DHL, XPO, Kuehne+Nagel, CEVA, and Wincanton — tracking OTIF rate, average delay days, cost impact by delay reason, and shipment volume across all UK origin ports.

Key Insight
XPO Logistics OTIF drops sharply in Q2 (April–June), with the carrier emerging as the highest average delay days across the year. Port Congestion and Carrier Delay account for the largest share of £518K total delay costs.

04

Logistics Operations Dashboard

Provides a full operational picture — inbound vs outbound volume by warehouse, cost-per-unit trends, labour hours, throughput rates, and shift-level efficiency — supporting both operational and financial reporting needs.

Key Insight
Cost-per-unit rises from ~£2.50 to ~£3.00 in November–December, reflecting Q4 peak pressure. Twilight shift consistently achieves the highest labour efficiency across all three warehouses.
